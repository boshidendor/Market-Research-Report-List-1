<p><p>The bending machine market consists of four primary types: hydraulic, mechanical, electric, and pneumatic. Hydraulic bending machines utilize fluid pressure for smooth and precise bending, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Mechanical machines rely on a system of levers and gears for power, suitable for lighter tasks. Electric bending machines offer energy efficiency and automation for consistent performance. Lastly, pneumatic machines use compressed air for quick operation, often favored in environments requiring rapid cycles and flexibility in operations.</p></p>
